My goal this semester is to merge these interests, exploring how high-level analytics can coexist with robust data privacy standards.

"Across 6,175 regular-season games from 2000–2023, home teams won 56.26% of the time. The advantage is real — but it appears weaker than it was in the early 2000s, and 2020 stands out as the most extreme outlier (49.80%). Data can describe these patterns, but we should be careful about claiming a single cause."
