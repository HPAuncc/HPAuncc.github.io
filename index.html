About this template

Unearth the essence of your professional story with this versatile data analytics portfolio, tailor-made for ambitious analysts and sports scientists. A narrative-driven canvas — free and adaptable — ready to broadcast your skills to the world.

Features at a Glance:

  • Home-Field Advantage Study. NFL analysis of 6,175 games (2000–2023), uncovering a 56.26% home win rate.
  • Data Ethics Writing. Blog exploring how high-level analytics can coexist with robust data privacy standards.
  • Interactive Visualizations. Charts and trend lines built with Python, pandas, matplotlib, and seaborn.
  • IBM Certified. IBM Data Science & Applied Data Science certifications (2025).
  • Professionally Linked. GitHub and LinkedIn profiles available for deeper exploration.

I am a Sports Analytics major at UNC Charlotte with a deep-rooted passion for the statistics that drive the games we love. While my technical focus is on optimizing performance and strategy in sports, I am equally driven by a passion for digital privacy. I believe that as we collect more data—on the field and off—we have a growing responsibility to handle that information ethically. My goal this semester is to merge these interests, exploring how high-level analytics can coexist with robust data privacy standards.
